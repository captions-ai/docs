Automatically remove pauses, dead space, and “uhms” with a few clicks.

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        **Automatically trim your video**

        1. Tap AI Trim on the bottom navigation
        2. Review each segment and adjust the trim accordingly
        3. Tap **Trim** to apply

        **Trim your video when importing**

        1. Import your video
        2. Before making your language selections and tap **Trim**
