---
title: "Manage Clips"
icon: "film"
description: Split, add, and delete clips
---

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        **Split your video**

        1. Tap on the timeline
        2. Scroll to the desired location
        3. Tap **Split**

        **Add a clip**

        - Tap on the timeline and adjust the timeline to where you'd like to insert a clip
        - Select **Split** and **Apply**
        - Tap the icon between your two split clips
        - Tap **Add**
        - Select your clip from your photo album
        <Tip>
            You can easily add a clip to the beginning or end of your project by tapping the plus button. After adding a clip, captions will not be added automatically. Learn how to add them [manually](/documentation/captions/add-captions)
        </Tip>
        **Delete a clip**

        1. Tap on the timeline
        2. Tap **Split** at the beginning and end of your desired location
        3. Tap on the segment to be removed and tap **Delete**
    </Tab>

    <Tab title="Desktop">
        **Split your video**

        1. Click on the timeline
        2. Click **Split** at the beginning and end of your desired location
        3. Click on the segment to be removed and click **Delete**

        **Delete a clip**

        1. Click on the timeline
        2. Click **Split** at the beginning and end of your desired location
        3. Click on the segment to be removed and click **Delete**
    </Tab>
</Tabs>
