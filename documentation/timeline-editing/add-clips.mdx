<Note>
    After adding a clip, captions will not be added automatically. Learn how to [add them manually](/documentation/captions/add-captions)
</Note>

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        **Add a clip**

        - Tap on the timeline and adjust the timeline to where you'd like to insert a clip
        - Select **Split** and **Apply**
        - Tap the icon between your two split clips
        - Tap **Add**
        - Select your clip from your photo album
        <Tip>
            You can easily add a clip to the beginning or end of your project by tapping the plus button
        </Tip>
    </Tab>
