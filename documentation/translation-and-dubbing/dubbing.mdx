---
title: "Dubbing"
---

Dubbing allows you to localize content and reach new audiences with a click of a button. After applying AI Dubbing, we apply a voice clone which will match the tone and cadence of the original speaker.

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        **Dub your video into a new language:**

        1. Import or record your video: [Manage Projects](/documentation/start-guide/create-a-project)
        2. Select the language that is being spoken
        3. Select the language that you’d like to translate to
        4. Tap **AI Dubbing** and **Apply**
    </Tab>

    <Tab title="Desktop">
        **Dub your video into a new language:**

        1. Import your video: [Manage Projects](/documentation/start-guide/create-a-project)
        2. Select the language that is being spoken
        3. Select the language that you’d like to translate to
        4. Click **AI Dubbing** and **Apply**
    </Tab>
</Tabs>
