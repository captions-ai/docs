Move, resize, and rotate your captions.

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        - Location - Use one finger to drag to a new location
        - Resize - Use two fingers and pinch in or out to make them smaller or larger
        - Rotate - Use two fingers and rotate
    </Tab>

    <Tab title="Desktop">
        - Location - Use your cursor to drag to a new location
        - Resize - Hover over a corner and drag your cursor
        - Rotate - Hover over a corner to rotate
    </Tab>
</Tabs>
