  To add a keyframe:
        <Steps>
            <Step title="First Step">
                Tap **Keyframes**.
            </Step>
            <Step title="Second Step">
                Adjust the timeline where you'd like to add a keyframe.
            </Step>
            <Step title="Third Step">
                Drag the caption to the desired location. You can also adjust the size of the captions by pinching with two fingers.
            </Step>
            <Step title="Fourth Step">
                Choose your animation curve.
            </Step>
            <Step title="Fifth Step">
                Tap **Apply**.
            </Step>
