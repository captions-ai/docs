Keyframes allow you adjust the position and size of your captions throughout your project.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS">
    **Add a keyframe:**

    1. Tap **Keyframes**.
    2. Adjust the timeline where you'd like to add a keyframe.
    3. Drag the caption to the desired location. You can also adjust the size of the captions by pinching with two fingers.
    4. Choose your animation curve and tap **Apply**
    ![How to add keyframes](https://s5.gifyu.com/images/SiVSW.gif)
