---
title: "Adjust Volume"
---

After your video has been dubbed and/or Lipdubbed to a new language, you may want to adjust the volume of a phrase.
<Note>
    This feature is only available for Dubbing and Lipdub
</Note>

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        **Adjust the volume of your Dubbed or Lipdubbed video**

        1. Tap on the segment
        2. Tap **Volume**
        3. Adjust the volume from 0-100%
    </Tab>

    <Tab title="Desktop">
        Coming soon to Desktop

</Tabs>
