After you've added captions automatically, hiding captions is helpful if you'd like to export without captions and deleting may be helpful if you'd like to simply delete all and add your own manually.

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        **Hide or delete a phrase**

        1. Tap on the phrase
        2. Tap **Hide** or Delete word or phrase

        To hide or delete individual words, see [Word Effects](/documentation/captions/word-effects)
    </Tab>
