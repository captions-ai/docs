## **Download**
        To download Captions for iOS, [tap here](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/id1541407007) while on your iOS device, search for "Captions" in the App Store, or scan the QR code below
        <Frame>
            <img src="/src/img/start-guide/ios-qr-code.png" />
        </Frame>

        ## **Availability**

        - **Devices** - Captions is available on iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch. While the app is available on iPad, please note that it is not currently optimized and you may experience some issues.
        - **Operating System** - You must be running iOS 15.0 or later to use Captions. [Learn more](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/update-ios-iph3e504502).
        - **Languages -** Currently, we offer the app in over 20 languages.

        ## **Keeping Captions up to date**

        ****Update to the latest version****

        1. Open the App Store
        2. Tap **Today** on the bottom left
        3. Tap your profile picture on the top right
        4. Pull down to refresh the page
        5. Tap **UPDATE** 
        Note: Please follow [these instructions](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202180) to enable automatic updates.
