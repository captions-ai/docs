        ## **Availability**

        - **Devices** - Captions is available on iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch. While the app is available on iPad, please note that it is not currently optimized and you may experience some issues.
        - **Operating System** - You must be running iOS 15.0 or later to use Captions. [Learn more](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/update-ios-iph3e504502).
        - **Languages -** Currently, we offer the app in over 20 languages.

        ## **Keeping Captions up to date**

        ****Update to the latest version****

        1. Open the App Store
        2. Tap **Today** on the bottom left
        3. Tap your profile picture on the top right
        4. Pull down to refresh the page
        5. Tap **UPDATE** 
        Note: Please follow [these instructions](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202180) to enable automatic updates.

    <Tab title="Desktop">
        Captions is available for download on Mac as well as access on any modern browser regardless of your operating system. The desktop app is receiving regular updates and will soon have all of the same features as iOS.

        - PC & Browsers - You can access the Captions app on a browser on [Windows, Mac, and Linux here](https://desktop.captions.ai/). Sign up to create a new account or sign in to restore your subscription.
        - MacOS - You can [download the Captions Mac app here](https://www.captions.ai/download). Sign up to create a new account or sign in to restore your subscription.

        ## Shortcuts

        **Global**

        - Spacebar : Play / Pause
        - Return : Edit spelling of selected word
        - ← : Move one word left
        - → : Move one word right
        - S: Supersize
        - E: Emphasize
        - Horizontal two finger scroll (trackpad) : Scrub video

        **In Split Mode**

        - Go-to next clip: Ctrl + Down / Cmd Down
        - Go-to previous clip: Ctrl + Up / Cmd Up
        - Delete current clip: Delete / Backspace
        - Split Clip at playhead: Ctrl + K / Cmd + K
